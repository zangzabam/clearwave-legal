Disconnect from QuickBooks

The ClearWave Growth Agent is an internal tool. To disconnect, an authorized ClearWave administrator deletes the stored OAuth token on the local server.

To revoke access on the Intuit side, visit My Apps in Intuit App Center and click "Disconnect" next to ClearWave Growth Agent.

To clear locally stored tokens: delete the file mcp_servers/quickbooks/token_store.db on the ClearWave server.
