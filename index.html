ClearWave Growth Agent

This is an internal marketing-analytics tool used exclusively by ClearWave Water Solutions, Inc. for its own business operations. It is not publicly distributed, does not serve end users, and is not available for download or signup.

The application connects only to ClearWave's own business data (QuickBooks Online, Jobber CRM, CallRail, Google Ads) to generate internal performance reports. No customer data is shared externally.
