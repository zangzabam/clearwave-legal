Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the ClearWave Growth Agent internal application (the "Application"), operated by ClearWave Water Solutions, Inc. ("ClearWave").

1. Scope

The Application is an internal tool. It does not collect data from the general public, does not offer a signup or account-creation flow, and is not marketed or distributed to external users.

2. Data we process

The Application retrieves and processes data from ClearWave's own third-party service accounts, solely to generate internal reports. This includes:

  • Financial data from ClearWave's QuickBooks Online account (transactions, P&L, vendor payments)
  • Customer records from ClearWave's Jobber CRM (client names, contact info, job records)
  • Call tracking data from ClearWave's CallRail account (call logs, source attribution)
  • Advertising data from ClearWave's Google Ads account (campaign performance)
  • Email marketing data from ClearWave's Mailchimp account (list stats, campaign reports)

3. How we use the data

All data is used exclusively by ClearWave for internal business purposes: marketing attribution, ROI analysis, campaign optimization, and operational reporting. The Application runs on local infrastructure under ClearWave's direct control.

4. Data sharing

ClearWave does not sell, rent, or share any of the above data with third parties. Data is not disclosed outside the company except as required by law.

5. Data retention

Data retrieved by the Application is stored locally on ClearWave's infrastructure for the duration needed to generate reports and improve operations. Original data remains in its source systems (QuickBooks, Jobber, etc.) under each service's own retention and access policies.

6. Security

Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted local storage. API keys and OAuth refresh tokens are never committed to version control or shared externally.

7. Customer data

Some data processed by the Application (from Jobber CRM and CallRail) pertains to ClearWave's actual customers. This customer data is handled in accordance with ClearWave's primary customer-facing privacy policy, available at clearwavewater.com. The Application does not collect any new customer data directly and does not alter customer-facing privacy commitments.

8. Your rights

Since the Application has no public users, there are no external data subjects whose rights would be implicated here. Customer rights under Virginia and applicable state privacy law are addressed under ClearWave's main privacy policy.
